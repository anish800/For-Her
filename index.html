Lately, you’ve been drifting... and I feel it like silence between two songs we used to love.
You’re still close in distance, but miles away in presence — and it breaks me quietly, piece by piece.
Maybe I failed to hold you right, or maybe I spoke less when I should’ve spoken love.
If there’s a way to reach you again — through time, through pain, through space — I’d take it barefoot, with every sorry I never said out loud.
Don’t just be my memory. Be my miracle. Because I still need you — in every version of my tomorrow.
